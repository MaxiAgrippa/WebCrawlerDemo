Emerging Tech Mobility All Hands on Tech – eSight electronic assisted glasses enhance eyesight Brian Jackson @brianjjackson Published: January 8th, 2019 Toronto-based eSight has been making its device that helps legally-blind people see, using a visor that relays an enhanced camera image to two screens right in front of the eyes. Now it’s slashed its price by 40%, which will allow it to reach thousands of more users at least. eSight wearer Rosa Henderson explains how the device made a difference in her life and how she’ll be helping onboard new users in the future.
