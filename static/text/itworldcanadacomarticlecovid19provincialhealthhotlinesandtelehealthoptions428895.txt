Digital Transformation Mobility Public Sector COVID-19: Provincial health hotlines and telehealth options Buckley Smith @BuckleySmith7 Published: March 25th, 2020 Accessing healthcare during a pandemic is not business as usual. Intensive care units and other critical healthcare infrastructure are dedicated to only the most crucial of cases, leaving many others scrambling for options. Those affected by COVID-19 are obviously high priority, but others with pre-existing medical conditions and those who suffer injuries or illnesses unrelated to the pandemic still need medical attention. So where do they turn to at a time like this? While we have seen a rise in the use of telehealth practises in recent years, not every province was as prepared for such a scenario as others, leaving a bit of a puzzle to decipher for those seeking care to when looking for the right telephone number to call or person to email. We have listed all available phone and video chat options (when available) by province below.
