Technology 15 most popular phones in Canada Here are Canada's most popular mobile handsets as of the middle of 2012. BlackBerrys and iPhones dominate the list. By Peter Nowak October 24, 2012 Gallery 15 most popular phones in Canada 1 / 16 The 15 phones in this gallery are the most popular mobile handsets in use by Canadians as of the middle of 2012, as determined by comScore. Six of them are iterations of the iPhone. Another six are BlackBerrys. Only two of the phones—the Samsung Galaxy Ace and the LG Optimus One—use Google’s Android operating system. But now the next-generaration smartphones are entering the market. Which ones will Canadians embrace?
